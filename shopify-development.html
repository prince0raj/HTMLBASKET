FAQ

1. What is Shopify Conversion?
This is the service whereby your designs are converted to a working online Shopify store. The finished site will be SEO friendly, cross-browser compatible and pixel perfect and will be usable immediately on completion.
2. Why should I opt for Shopify?
Shopify is probably the biggest and most popular content management system (CMS) available, and as it’s a CMS you don’t need to know how to code to be able to make change sot the site once we’ve built it. There are also lots of plugins and extensions, making it one of the most customisable on the market.
3. How do I use Shopify?
Shopify is software that is run over the internet (web based), so you don’t have to install anything. You simply go to the Shopify website and login to access your store. It’s that simple.
4. How do you do the conversion from Design to Shopify?
It’s all really quite easy for our experts. We just need your Design files, or other layered image, and we then do our research to ensure that your design will fit to a Shopify template. It’s a little like using blueprints to complete a construction project – your Design files are the blueprints. We do the building.
5. Do I need an expert to change my Shopify store design?
No. Shopify is designed to be user friendly and to allow inexperienced web users to manage their store with ease. You are able to customise the appearance of your store with a few easy clicks, and there are new designs added to Shopify regularly.
6. Am I able to use my own domain with Shopify?
Yes, you can use your on domain name with your Shopify store, so that when a customer types in your domain, it directs them to your Shopify store. To change the domain of your Shopify store, simply go to your Shopify store, then head to Preferences, Domains and register any domain you like (so long as it’s available). Simple huh? You can also use an existing domain even if it’s registered through another domain name provider. This is a little more complicated, but you can see how to do this here http://docs.shopify.com/manual/settings/domains/third-party-domains.
7. What languages does Shopify support?
Your storefront can be set to any language you like. The admin panel however is in English.
8. What payment methods can I accept?
Accepted payment methods via Shopify are almost limitless. You can expect to be able to accept almost any payment method. Visit http://www.shopify.com/payment-gateways for more info.
9. What do I need to set up to start selling my products?
All you need to be able to start selling with a Shopify store is a Shopify account, products and a debit or credit card to pay for the recurring Shopify store fees.
10. Am I able to accept payments manually?
Yes, you are able to accept cheque and postal orders as methods of payment, or even direct BACS transfers. There are no limited to accepted payment methods.
