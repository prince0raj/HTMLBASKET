FAQ

1. Is it important to have a responsive website?
Absolutely yes! Today more than 50% of the website visitors access websites using hand held devices. If your website is not designed using responsive technology then your visitors will have a tough time browsing your website in hand held devices with small displays. They may quickly leave your website, frustrated not finding what they want. Responsive technology will automatically render pages that are suitable to the device used. This way, you do not have to worry about losing visitors.
2. Does responsive website have any special positive effects on SEO?
Search engines like Google give special weightage to websites that use responsive technology. Therefore, creating responsive websites will certainly have positive effect on your website’s SEO. Moreover, when you have a responsive website, you will just have one URL per page regardless of the device used to access it as opposed to a non-responsive website that will have multiple URLs pointing to the same page.

Yes, we create cross browser compatible pages and not only that we test all the pages on all the latest browsers that are available at the time of coding.
