FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

Our frequently asked questions help to answer some of our most common queries and can help you to ascertain if our service is right for you. If you have a question that is not covered here, please contact us via the contact page.

What is Responsive HTML conversion?
Responsive HTML conversion is the term used for a service whereby you supply a Adobe Photoshop , Illustrator , Adobe XD , Sketch, Figma, Invision design and we convert this to HTML or CSS coding that is suitable for use on the web or internet. Our talented coders manually code web pages and also deal with slicing during the process of converting your files. All codes provided by HTMLBASKET comply with W3C standards and are guaranteed suitable for cross browser use.
Is your team in-house or do you outsource?
All of the work that we do is done by our programmers, developers, coders and designers In-house and we have a large team so that we can cope with large orders and projects. That means that all our experts have been pre-screened in our rigorous employment process.

What are your customer service response turnaround times?
We aim to reply to all queries within 12-24 hours but please allow up to 48 hours as we may take slightly longer during busy periods.
Who is able to use your Responsive HTML service?
Anybody can use our service, however it is most commonly used by web designers, web developers, bloggers who want to convert their designs, and website owners who wish to enjoy more customisation options with a new website design. We also work with some web design companies who want to regularly outsource their coding. But in fact, anybody at all who has a PSD or Illustration or Sketch file that they want to be coded can contact us to make use of our talented coders.
Is your coding manual or done by software?
All coding done by HTMLBASKET is done manually by our team of expert coders and we do not use any software to do all or any part of our image conversion coding.
How long do you keep backup files for?
We keep backups of the files that we use and create for a maximum of 6 months after the completed project is delivered. After this time the backup files will be deleted and cannot be recovered by us. We therefore urge clients and customers to keep their own backup.
What formats/source files are you able to convert?
We can work with Photoshop, Illustrator, Adobe Reader, Fireworks, XD, EPS, TIF, JPEG, PDF, Sketch and Figma, Invision design files for conversion projects. If you have another file type that you wish to work with, please contact us and we will respond within 12-24 hours.
